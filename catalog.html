John Evans Fiifi Atta Mills

Ghanaian politician and legal scholar who served as President of Ghana from 2009 until his death in 2012.

Success Story: He was inaugurated on 7 January 2009, having defeated the governing party candidate Nana Akufo-Addo in the 2008 Ghanaian presidential election. He was previously the Vice-President from 1997 to 2001 under President Jerry Rawlings, and he contested unsuccessfully in the 2000 and 2004 presidential elections as the candidate of the National Democratic Congress (NDC).
